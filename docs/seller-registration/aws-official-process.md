# AWS Marketplace Seller Registration - Official Process

Based on AWS official documentation and workshop materials:
- [AWS Marketplace Seller Registration Process](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/marketplace/latest/userguide/registration-process.html)
- [AWS Marketplace Seller Workshop](https://catalog.workshops.aws/mpseller/en-US/pre-requisite-register-as-seller)

## Overview

The AWS Marketplace seller registration process involves several steps to verify your business and set up payment processing. This is a one-time process that typically takes 3-5 business days to complete.

## Prerequisites

### Account Requirements
- Valid AWS account in good standing
- Account must be at least 30 days old
- Valid payment method on file with AWS
- Root user or IAM user with appropriate permissions

### Business Requirements
- Legitimate business entity
- Valid business information and documentation
- Ability to provide customer support
- Compliance with AWS Marketplace policies

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Access AWS Marketplace Management Portal

1. Sign in to the AWS Management Console
2. Navigate to AWS Marketplace Management Portal
3. Go to: https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/management/seller-registration
4. Click "Register as a seller"

### Step 2: Create Business Profile

**Required Information:**
- Legal business name
- Business type (Corporation, LLC, Partnership, Sole Proprietorship, etc.)
- Business address (must match tax documents)
- Business phone number
- Business email address
- Tax identification number (EIN for US, equivalent for other countries)
- Primary contact information

**Business Types Supported:**
- Corporation (C-Corp, S-Corp)
- Limited Liability Company (LLC)
- Partnership (General, Limited)
- Sole Proprietorship
- Non-profit organization
- Government entity

### Step 3: Create Public Profile

**Required Information:**
- Company name (public-facing)
- Company description (marketing copy)
- Company logo (uploaded to S3)
- Website URL
- Support email address
- Support phone number
- Company address (public)

**Optional Information:**
- Additional company URLs
- Social media links
- Company size
- Founded year

### Step 4: Tax Information

**US-based Sellers:**
- Complete W-9 form
- Provide Tax ID (EIN or SSN)
- Select tax classification
- Backup withholding certification

**International Sellers:**
- Complete appropriate W-8 form (W-8BEN, W-8BEN-E, etc.)
- Provide foreign tax identification
- Tax treaty benefits (if applicable)
- Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 status

**Required Documents:**
- Completed tax form (W-9 or W-8)
- Government-issued ID
- Proof of business registration (if applicable)

### Step 5: Banking Information

**Required Information:**
- Bank name and address
- Account type (Checking or Savings)
- Account number
- Routing number (US) or SWIFT code (International)
- Account holder name (must match business name)

**Verification Process:**
- AWS will make small test deposits
- Verify deposit amounts in your account
- Confirm amounts in the portal
- Process typically takes 1-2 business days

### Step 6: Identity Verification

**Individual Verification:**
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
- Phone number verification

**Business Verification:**
- Articles of incorporation or business license
- Proof of business address
- Authorized signatory verification

### Step 7: Select Disbursement Method

**Available Methods:**
- **ACH Direct Deposit** (US only, recommended)
  - Fastest processing (2-3 business days)
  - Lowest fees
  - Automatic processing
- **Wire Transfer** (International)
  - 3-5 business days processing
  - Higher fees
  - Manual processing for large amounts
- **Check** (US only)
  - 7-10 business days processing
  - Highest fees
  - Physical mail delivery

**Disbursement Schedule:**
- Monthly disbursements (default)
- Minimum threshold: $1 USD
- Processing on the 15th of each month
- 30-day payment terms

## Validation and Review Process

### AWS Review Timeline
- **Initial Review:** 1-2 business days
- **Document Verification:** 2-3 business days
- **Banking Verification:** 1-2 business days
- **Total Process:** 3-7 business days

### Common Review Items
- Business legitimacy verification
- Tax document accuracy
- Banking information validation
- Identity verification
- Compliance with AWS policies

### Possible Delays
- Incomplete or inaccurate information
- Missing required documents
- Banking verification issues
- Additional documentation requests
- Compliance review requirements

## Country-Specific Requirements

### United States
- EIN or SSN required
- W-9 tax form
- US bank account for ACH
- State business registration (if applicable)

### India
- PAN (Permanent Account Number)
- GST registration number
- Indian bank account
- W-8BEN-E form for tax treaty benefits
- Additional KYC documentation

### European Union
- VAT registration number
- Local tax identification
- SEPA-compatible bank account
- W-8BEN-E form
- Country-specific business registration

### Other Countries
- Local tax identification number
- Appropriate W-8 form
- Local bank account or USD account
- Business registration documents
- Additional compliance requirements

## Tips for Success

### Best Practices
1. **Complete Information:** Provide all required details accurately
2. **Quality Documents:** Submit clear, legible documents
3. **Consistent Information:** Ensure all details match across forms
4. **Responsive Communication:** Respond quickly to AWS requests
5. **Professional Profile:** Create compelling public profile

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Incomplete business information
- Mismatched names across documents
- Invalid banking details
- Poor quality document uploads
- Delayed responses to verification requests
