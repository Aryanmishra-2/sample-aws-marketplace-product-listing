# AWS Marketplace Seller Registration - India Specific Requirements

## Overview

Indian businesses and individuals can register as AWS Marketplace sellers with specific requirements and considerations for the Indian market.

## Prerequisites for Indian Sellers

### Business Requirements
- Valid Indian business entity or individual
- PAN (Permanent Account Number) - mandatory
- GST registration (for businesses with turnover > ₹20 lakhs)
- Indian bank account
- Valid business address in India

### Account Requirements
- AWS account with Indian billing address
- Valid Indian payment method
- Account in good standing for 30+ days

## Step-by-Step Process for Indian Sellers

### Step 1: Business Profile Setup

**Required Information:**
- Legal business name (as per PAN/GST registration)
- Business type:
  - Private Limited Company
  - Public Limited Company
  - Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)
  - Partnership Firm
  - Sole Proprietorship
  - Individual/Freelancer
- Registered business address
- Business phone number (+91 format)
- Business email address
- PAN number (mandatory)
- GST number (if applicable)

**Additional Documents:**
- Certificate of Incorporation (for companies)
- Partnership Deed (for partnerships)
- GST Registration Certificate
- PAN Card copy
- Address proof (utility bill, rent agreement)

### Step 2: Tax Information for Indian Sellers

**Tax Classification:**
- **Individual:** Use PAN as tax identifier
- **Business Entity:** Use PAN and GST number
- **Foreign Tax Benefits:** W-8BEN-E form for treaty benefits

**Required Tax Documents:**
- **W-8BEN-E Form:** For claiming tax treaty benefits
- **PAN Card:** Mandatory for all sellers
- **GST Certificate:** For registered businesses
- **TDS Certificate:** If applicable
- **Form 15CA/15CB:** For foreign remittances (if required)

**Tax Treaty Benefits:**
- India-US tax treaty provisions
- Reduced withholding tax rates
- Proper documentation required
- Annual recertification needed

### Step 3: Banking Information

**Supported Bank Account Types:**
- Current Account (recommended for businesses)
- Savings Account (for individuals)
- NRE/NRO accounts (for NRIs)

**Required Banking Details:**
- Bank name and branch
- Account holder name (must match PAN name)
- Account number
- IFSC code
- SWIFT code (for international transfers)
- Bank address

**Verification Process:**
- AWS will initiate test deposits
- Verify amounts in your account
- Confirm in AWS portal
- Process takes 2-3 business days

### Step 4: Identity Verification

**Individual Sellers:**
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card (recommended)
- Passport or Driving License
- Address proof (Aadhaar, utility bill, bank statement)
- Phone number verification (OTP)

**Business Sellers:**
- Authorized signatory's PAN Card
- Aadhaar Card of signatory
- Board resolution (for companies)
- Power of attorney (if applicable)
- Business registration documents

### Step 5: Compliance Requirements

**GST Compliance:**
- GST registration if turnover exceeds threshold
- Regular GST return filing
- Input tax credit management
- E-way bill compliance (if applicable)

**Income Tax Compliance:**
- Regular income tax return filing
- TDS compliance on payments
- Advance tax payments (if applicable)
- Audit requirements (for higher turnover)

**Foreign Exchange Compliance:**
- FEMA compliance for foreign receipts
- RBI reporting requirements
- Export documentation
- Repatriation of export proceeds

## Payment and Disbursement

### Disbursement Methods for India

**Wire Transfer (Primary Method):**
- Processing time: 3-5 business days
- Minimum threshold: $1 USD
- Currency: USD (converted to INR by bank)
- Fees: Wire transfer charges apply

**Payment Schedule:**
- Monthly disbursements
- Payment on 15th of each month
- 30-day payment terms
- Automatic currency conversion

### Tax Implications

**Withholding Tax:**
- US withholding tax: 30% (reduced to 10% with treaty)
- Indian tax on foreign income
- TDS on payments received
- Double taxation relief available

**Tax Planning:**
- Claim treaty benefits with W-8BEN-E
- Maintain proper documentation
- Regular tax compliance
- Professional tax advice recommended
